Zoo Tycoon [Beta]

Project Duration: 6/02/2022 - Present

This is one of my most recent and well organized project in game development. I implemented everything i've learned from class and tried to stay consistent with the structure of the game files.

Whats the game about?

You will spawn at your own plot, in wich you can load a already existing game or create a new one.
Your goal is to build a zoo with stalls and animal habbits (wich is a mecanic thats still in development.) Npc's will come and visit your park, they will choose a stall or path to visit randomly from a table thats filled with all available items in the park.
If an NPC cant reach its target 5 points are substracted from their "liking" variable wich can be between 0 or 100 the higher the number, The lower the change they leave. When they are able to reach the target they'll walk up to it and perform an action based on what their target was.
For instance when they are at a stall they'll buy something, wich could be either an accecory or food, bought accecory's are show in game on the NPC.
